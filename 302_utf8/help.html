In the assignment page, is my work saved automatically?

No.When submitting an assignment, you may save your work by copying your writingand pasting in a Word file, for example, to save it in your hard-drive or afloppy disk. You have the option to print to save your work directly from theAssignments page by clicking the “Print” button at the bottom of the page aftersubmitting the assignment. Be sure to receive the sent message forconfirmation. It is your responsibility to submit the assignment on time.

What should I do if Japanese characters are displayed improperly in Microsoft Internet Explorer?

  1. Go to the TOOL > INTERNET OPTIONS > LANGUAGE and add “Japanese.”
  2. Make sure your operation system has the Japanese language package installed.
  3. The CD works best with Windows 2000 and later versions. Most problems are associated with Windows 98 and older versions.
  4. Macintosh computers also work best with the latest versions of Mac OS.

Do I need to see the translation?

No.T he mental process of extrapolating the meaning from the text isimportant. Use only when you come across some difficult part in the text andonly as the last resort.
