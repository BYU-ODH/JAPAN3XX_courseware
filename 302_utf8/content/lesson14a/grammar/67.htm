〜のは無理もないことだ:
It is understandable 〜

Explanation: A speaker will often use this pattern to express an unfazed reaction: "It makes sense that....", "It's only logical that....", "It's quite understandable that....", etc.

例. そんなに好きなんだから、一緒に行きたがるのは無理もないことだ。
It is quite understandable that she really wants to go with him since she loves him so.
