V (BaseII) ながら:
while V

Explanation: When ながら follows an active verb, the sentence expresses actions which take place simultaneously.

例1. 食べながら話してはいけません。
You mustn't talk with food in your mouth.

例2. ラジオを聞きながら勉強した。
He studied while listening to the radio.

When ながら follows a noun, an adjective, or a stative verb, it expresses a situation contrary to that state, "or even though ..."

例3. 病気ながら、頑張った。
He worked hard even though he was sick.

例4. 日が照っていながら、寒いなんておかしいね。
It's strange that it's cold even though the sun is shining.
