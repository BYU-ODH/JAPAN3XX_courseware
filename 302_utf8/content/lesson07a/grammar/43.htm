V ていく:
become V

Explanation: V ていく usually describes the process of becoming or growing. It is a process that will continue after a certain time. V ていく can be contrasted with V てくる in that V ていく gives the feeling of process growing away from the speaker, thus becoming more objective or impersonal. On the other hand, V てくる involves the speaker or the process approaching the speaker.

例1. 勝敗は明白になっていく。
The outcome will become clear.

例2. 私の病気は悪くなっていくばかりだ。
My illness is just getting worse.
