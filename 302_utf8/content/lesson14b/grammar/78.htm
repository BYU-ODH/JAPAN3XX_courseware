X とする: It is supposed that 〜

Explanation: This form indicates supposition on the part of the speaker.

例. x + 2 をAとすると、3 (x + 2) は3Aになる。

This phrase is often used to avoid the strong assertion of the speaker by transferring to the person who made the statement. Hence, here the statement "We have entered the global civilization" is the professor's assertion and not the author's.
