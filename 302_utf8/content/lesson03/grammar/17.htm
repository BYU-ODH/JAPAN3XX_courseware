〜 のではないか:
probably;
〜 , right?

Explanation: This structure comes from a rhetorical question: 〜, isn't that right? It is a desire to express some uncertainty of the speaker.“Probably” is the closest to an English equivalent. It is often followed by the expression“思われます.”

例. 日本の世の中はこれからもますます変わって行くのではないか。
I think that Japanese society will probably continue to change even more.
