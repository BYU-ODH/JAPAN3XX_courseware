V (Base II) かける:
start to;
begin to

Explanation: This expression gives the feeling as if the verb has started and it was left hanging in the middle; hence the meaning "started to V."

例1. 食べかけて外へ行ってはいけません。
You must not go out while you are eating.

例2. 走りかけていたバスに乗ろうとした。
He tried to board a bus that had begun to move.
