N だけでなく:
not only N

Explanation: X can be a noun, adjective, な nominal, or a verb. In other words, だけ acts like a noun (pseudonoun) to which X acts as its modifier.

例1. 漢字だけでなく文法や発言の仕方を勉強しないとダメだ。
You must not only study kanji, but you must study grammar and pronunciation too.

例2. わたしだけでなくほかの人もそう言っていた。
I'm not the only one who said that.
