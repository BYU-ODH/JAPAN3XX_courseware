〜なら:
if (assertive)

Explanation: An if-clause with なら usually indicates an assertion, meaning "if you tell me 〜, then . . . ." Hence, this clause is used often with subjective statements.

例1. 勉強したいなら図書館へ行きなさい。
If you want to study, go to the library.

例2. 自分に都合がいいのなら何でもするたちなのです。
He's the type that does anything if it's convenient for him.
