〜せい:
because of 〜; owing to 〜; fault of 〜

Explanation: せい functions like a noun. If another noun precedes it, it takes の (keiyō-dōshi takes な); if it is an adjective or verb it takes nothing. It ususally connotes the speaker’s negative or unfortunate feeling towards the cause. cf.〜おかげ

例. 日本で育ったせいもあり、英語があまりよくできない。
Partially because he grew up in Japan, he can't speak English very well.
