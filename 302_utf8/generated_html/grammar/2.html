〜 のが (は) X だ:
the one who/that 〜 is/was X

Explanation: の here refers to what comes in X. X can refer to people, things, places, times, reasons, etc. X is usually a noun, a noun phrase, or a noun plus a preposition.

例1. 皿をこわしたのは田中です。
It is Tanaka that broke the plate.

例2. 日本へ来たのは勉強するためで、あそぶためではありません。
I came to Japan to study, not to play.
