〜ようだ:
It seems as though

Explanation: 〜 can be any sentence. If it ends with a noun, の is inserted and with na-nominal (keiyodoshi) なis inserted before よう. This structure is used to show the speaker's conjecture "It seems to me."

例. 小野小町は大変な美人だったようです。
Ono Komachi seemes to have been a rare beauty.
