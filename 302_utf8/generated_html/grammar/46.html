〜ものの:
even though 〜

Explanation: 〜 can be a complete sentence. 〜ものの means, "What is expressed by 〜 is true, but . . . ." It expresses the speaker's admitting to 〜 with reservations.

例. もう悪いことはしないとは言うものの、そう簡単に信用すべきではない。
Even though he says he won't do anymore bad things, we shouldn't trust him that easily.
