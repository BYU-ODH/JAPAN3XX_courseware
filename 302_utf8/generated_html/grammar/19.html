〜ような気がする:
feel like (that);
get the feeling

Explanation: This structure is used to express the feeling or impression of the speaker. Often it is something uncertain or contrary to the fact, but the speaker feels as though something were happening.

例1. なんだか彼がまだ生きているような気がする。
Somehow I feel that he is still alive.

例2. 気が遠くなってくるような気がする。
I feel like I’m going to faint.
