N にとって:
for (to) N

Explanation: N usually refers to a human. The content of the sentence applies to N, which is marked by にとって. Translated, にとって could, in this case, be "as for N" or "for N," as in the sentence "For me, the most delicious treat is ice cream."

例1. 子供達にとってそれはクリスマスのようでした。
That was just like Christmas to the children.

例2. 生徒達にとってテストはとても難しかったようです。
The test seemed to be very difficult for the students.
