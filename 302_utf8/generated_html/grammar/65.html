…ぞ:
emphasis (male speech)

Explanation: This structure is used to emphasize that the information is the speaker's. It is so strong that a female speaker would not use it. This final particle conveys a certain cockiness or roughness by the speaker.

例1. 先に行くぞ。
I'll go first.

例2. そんなことをするとおこるぞ。
If you do that, I'll get angry.
