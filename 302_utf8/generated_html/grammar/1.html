Nを基に:
based upon N

Explanation: もと is sometimes written in kanji 基 "foundation or basis," or in another case 元 "source or origin." The meaning of the sentence becomes much clearer when it is written in kanji; the noun preceding 基に is the base of the action of the main clause.

例1. 自分の経験（けいけん）を基に本を書いた。
He wrote a book based upon his own experiences.

例2. いただいたお金を基に商売をしてみます。
I will go into business using the money I have received.
