〜につれ:
along with〜

Explanation: This pattern comes from the verb 連れる "to take along." ~ must be a non-past verb action or process. As this process 〜 occurs, something else happens.

例1. 日がたつにつれ、だんだん痛みがうすらいでいった。
With each passing day the pain lessens.

例2. 人口がふえるにつれ、町の治安が悪化した。
With the increase in population, the public peace in cities has worsened.
