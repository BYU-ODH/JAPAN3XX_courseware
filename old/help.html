FAQ

 

In the assignment page, is my work saved automatically?

          No. When submitting an assignment, you may save your work by copying your writing and pasting in a Word file, for example, to save it in your hard-drive or a floppy disk. You have the option to print to save your work directly from the Assignments page by clicking the “Print” button at the bottom of the page after submitting the assignment. Be sure to receive the sent message for confirmation. It is your responsibility to submit the assignment on time.

 

What should I do if Japanese characters are displayed improperly in Microsoft Internet Explorer?

  1. Go to the TOOL > INTERNET OPTIONS > LANGUAGE and add “Japanese.”
  2. Make sure your operation system has the Japanese language package installed.
  3. The CD works best with Windows 2000 and later versions. Most problems are associated with Windows 98 and older versions.
  4. Macintosh computers also work best with the latest versions of Mac OS.

 

Do I need to see the translation?

           No. The mental process of extrapolating the meaning from the text is important. Use only when you come across some difficult part in the text and only as the last resort.

 

I can’t understand the explanation of a specific word or sentence on the CD.

You may talk to your instructor outside of the class or preferably in class.
