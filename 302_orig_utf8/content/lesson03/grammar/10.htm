〜 に過ぎない:
It is nothing more than N;
only N

Explanation: 〜 is usually a noun or a noun phrase, but can be a sentence.

例1. これはたんに私の個人の意見に過ぎませんが、それをするのは無理ではないかと思われます。
This is just my personal opinion, but I would think that it might be impossible to do that.

例2. 漢字ができると言ってもやさしいのが少し読めるに過ぎない。
Even though they say they can read kanji, all they can read are simple characters.
