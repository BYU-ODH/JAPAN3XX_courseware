なるべく 〜:
as (adj, adv) as possible;
as much as possible

Explanation: なるべく modifies predicates; hence, 〜 is usually an adjective, an adverb, or a verb. It often goes with an expression of volition, request, command, or suggestion.

例1. なるべく早く来て下さい。
Please come as quickly as possible.

例2. なるべく易しいクイズを作って下さい。
Please make the quiz as simple as possible.
