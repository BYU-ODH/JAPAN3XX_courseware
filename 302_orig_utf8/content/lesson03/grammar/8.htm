〜と言っても:
Even though we claim 〜

Explanation: This structure comes from a more common structure 〜ても, even if. Often, 〜 is a general statement.

例1. きれいだと言ってもクレオパトラほどじゃない。
You might say she is pretty, but she is not as beautiful as Cleopatra.

例2. 帰れと言っても帰らないから仕方がない。
Even though I tell him to go home, he doesn't, so there's not much I can do.
