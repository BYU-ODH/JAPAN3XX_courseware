X どころか Y:
not only X, but also Y;
far from being X

Explanation: This structure is used to indicate the speaker's denial of X, which might be inferred and expected from the context. Translated, it means, "You might expect X; far from being X, it is Y."

例1. うれしいどころか、悲しくてどうしていいのかわかりません。
Far from happy, I am so sad I don't know what to do.

例2. 話はまとまるどころか、かえって意見がばらばらになるばかり。
Far from reaching a settlement, everyone's opinions got all scattered.
