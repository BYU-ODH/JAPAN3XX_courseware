〜ことでしょう:
probably

Explanation: 〜こと makes the sentence more abstract. In other words, the speaker makes the statement more detached from him or her. Oftentimes it is used in narration. With でしょう the speakers conjecture added, it is a very detached, sometimes polite conjecture of the speaker.

例1. 生活もこれからだんだんよくなっていくことでしょう。
From here on, his life will gradually improve.

例2. そんなに大勢の人が集まったらきっとうるさかったことでしょう。
If there were that many people there, it must have been very noisy.
