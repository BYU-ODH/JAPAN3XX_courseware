X をY に変える:
change X into Y

Explanation: The verb 変える is a transitive verb and takes a direct object marked with を. The noun marked with に is the result of the change.

例1. テレビのチャンネルをもっとおもしろいのに変えてよ。
Change the television program to a more interesting one.

例2. 朝日新聞を読売新聞に変えました。
I changed my subscription from the Asahi newspaper to the Yomiuri.
