〜とでも言った方がいい:
It is better to call it something 〜;
We might (could) call it〜

Explanation: This structure comes from 〜た方がいい: It is better to 〜. 言うhere is more general, although the whole sentence is a very subjective statement.

例. ディズニーランドは子供の遊園地と言うよりも大人の遊園地とでも言った方がいい。
Disneyland could just as easily be called an amusement park for adults as one for children.
