〜のではないか:
I'll bet〜;
〜, isn't it?

Explanation: This is a rhetorical device to be placed at the end of statement 〜, meaning "Isn’t it 〜," or "Yes it is 〜." It emphatically expresses the author's opinion that 〜 is true.

例. 父が生きていたらこんなことにはならなかったのではないか。
I'll bet if my father had been living, this kind of thing would not have happened.
