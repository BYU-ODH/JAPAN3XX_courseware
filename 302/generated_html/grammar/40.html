〜ていた…past:
was (were) V-ing;
had done

Explanation: When the 〜ていた structure occurs within a sentence which has as a main clause the past tense of a verb, it indicates that the action was taking place or had taken place at the time of the realization of the main clause.

例1. 夕食を食べていた時に電話がかかってきた。
While I was eating dinner, I got a telephone call.

例2. 本を読んでいたので講義がよくわかった。
Because I had read the book, I understood the lecture.
