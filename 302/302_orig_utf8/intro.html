This is the study aid for Intermediate Reader: Japanese History and Culture . This CD includes the following features:

  • The full text of your textbook with translation
  • Recordings of native Japanese speakers reading
  • Hyperlinks to grammar and vocabulary explanations
  • Hyperlinks to kanji animations to show stroke order
  • Web-based assignment submission

    • The content is divided into four units. The four title screens of Lesson Index page represent the four distinctive ages by pictures: the beginning of the country, the establishment of samurai society, the expansion of samurai society, and the modernization of Japan. Each unit box has four to eight stories. Click on a title to start.

    This CD is designed to enrich your study with audio and visual materials. This helps you absorb the information presented here more thoroughly and retain it longer. We suggest that you click on kanji and sound files as frequently as possible. The CD is not intended to add to your study load, but we believe that the time you spend CD-surfing will be beneficial when you really think about each story and internalize the objects of the lesson. You should not force yourself into mere memorization; rather, surf around to absorb the material in a more relaxed way.

    We hope you enjoy studying Intermediate Reader: Japanese History and Culture and have fun using this CD.
