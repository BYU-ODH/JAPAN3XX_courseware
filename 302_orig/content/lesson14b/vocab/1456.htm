Definition: A city of west-central Honshu, Japan, north-northeast of Ōsaka. Founded in the eighth century, it has long been a cultural, artistic, and religious center. Kyōto was Japan's capital from 794 until 1869, although its political importance declined after the rise of the shoguns (1192).
