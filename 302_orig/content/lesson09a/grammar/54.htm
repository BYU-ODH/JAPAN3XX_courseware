〜たちだ : disposition; quality; nature

Explanation: The clause 〜 before たち must be a verbal clause. In other words, nouns and adjectives cannot modify たち. Generally speaking, たち implies an undesirable or negative disposition, but that is not always the case as in examples 1 and 2.

例1. あの人は何でも一生懸命やるたちだからきっと成功する。
He tends to do everything diligently, so he'll surely succeed.

例2. あの人はそういうたちだから許してあげて下さい。
Please forgive him. It's just his nature.
